Frequently asked questions

How does Strata App work?

Strata App reads Apple Health data locally on your device and enables export to structured formats such as CSV, JSON, or Markdown.

Do my data leave the device?

Data access and export are local. If you use this support page or contact options, the privacy notes on this page apply to those interactions.

Is Strata App a medical diagnosis app?

No. Strata App provides data access and export, but no diagnosis, treatment recommendation, or medical interpretation.

Can I evaluate exported files with local AI tools?

Yes. That is a core use case. Structured exports can be processed with local LLM tools while keeping full data ownership.

Important notes

  • The app does not replace medical, legal, or privacy advice.
  • Health data should always be reviewed consciously before sharing.
  • Strata App is designed as a data ownership and export tool, not as a diagnostic system.

App privacy

  • The app runs locally on the device.
  • There is no mandatory cloud processing for access or export.
  • The app uses no tracking, no ads, and no user profiles.
  • There is no registration, no account, and no subscription for the core workflow.
  • Export files are created only on explicit user action.

Privacy

Strata App is designed as a local app for iPhone and iPad. Access to Apple Health data and export actions run on-device.

If you send a support request by email or contact form, the information you provide is used solely to process your request.

This support page uses no cookies and no user accounts. If you use external contact channels, the privacy policies of those providers also apply.
